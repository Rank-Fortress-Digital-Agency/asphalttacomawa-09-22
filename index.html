Why Should You Never Strip Your Parking Lot?


You might think the best way to clear snow and ice from a parking lot is to do it yourself. But stripping a parking lot can be very dangerous, and it’s not something you should try to do on your own. Here’s why:

Read Post

You could damage your parking lot.


Stripping a parking lot can be tough on the pavement, and you could do severe damage if you’re not careful. It’s not uncommon for people to strip their parking lots so severely that they must have them wholly repaved. If you want to avoid this costly mistake, you can do a few things.

The first thing you need to do is make sure that you have the right equipment. A power washer is a must, and you’ll also need a stiff brush or broom. You should also invest in some good quality stripping agents. These will help remove any pavement build-up and make it easier to strip.

It’s dangerous.


Not only is stripping a parking lot hard on the pavement, but it can also be dangerous for you and anyone else. You could seriously hurt yourself if you slip and fall while stripping the parking lot. That’s why it’s essential to take some safety precautions before you start stripping the parking lot.

First, make sure that you have the right equipment for the job. You’ll need a power washer, a stripper, and a squeegee. If you don’t have a power washer, you can rent one from your local hardware store.

Next, clear the area around the parking lot that you’ll be working in. This will help prevent accidents. Finally, always wear protective clothing when stripping a parking lot. This includes gloves, eye protection, and long sleeves and pants.

It’s against the law.


In many states, striping a parking lot is actually against the law. That’s because it can be so dangerous and because it can damage the pavement. You could get a ticket or even be fined if you strip your parking lot.

So, to avoid getting into trouble, it’s best to leave the stripping to the professionals. They have the equipment and the training to do it safely and without damaging your parking lot. Plus, they can usually get the job done much faster than you could, so you’ll also save time.

In short, unless you are a professional parking lot stripper, it’s best to leave the job to someone else. It’s not worth risking your safety or damaging your property. Just hire a professional to take care of it and save yourself the hassle.
